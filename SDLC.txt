SDLC(Properly defined methodology/Stages of develpoment of desired product)
1. Requirement gathering (Product owner gets requirements in a document called BRD
2. Analysis (Dev lead & QA lead take BRD as entry criteria and will prepre FSD as exit criteria)
3. Design/Planning (Study BRD as entry criteria and release for HLD/LLD as exit criteria)
4. Devlopment/Coding (Approved BRD, HLD/LLD and exit criteria as exe files.)
5. Testing (BRD,HLD/LLD, Deployed Build and exit crietria is test cases, defects and closure)
6. Deployment/Maintenance (production and support team)
